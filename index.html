I am a final-year student (integrated B.Tech + M.Tech) in the Department of Electrical Engineering at the Indian Institute of Technology, Bombay. I am part of the Computational Speech and Language Technologies Lab (CSALT) and am fortunate to be advised by Prof. Preethi Jyothi, Department of Computer Science & Engineering, IIT Bombay. I have also spent a summer as an intern at Adobe Research, India in 2019.

My research interests lie in the fields of Machine Learning, Natural Language Processing, and Speech Recognition. I maintain a list of my publications and research projects under the Research tab.

I will be joining the PhD program in Computer Science at UNC Chapel Hill in Fall 2021 where my research will be focussed on machine learning and natural language processing. I will be working with Prof. Mohit Bansal and will be a part of the MURGe-Lab and the broader UNC-NLP group.
