Research

My research interests broadly lie in the fields of Machine Learning, Automatic Speech Recognition and Speech Processing. I also have an interest in time-series and sequence-to-sequence tasks. Recently, I have also been interested in explainability and interpretability of black box models in Automatic Speech Recognition and Natural Language Processing.
